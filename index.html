About Us

Maharashtra Special Housing Yojna

Maharashtra Special Housing Yojna is a government-supported residential housing initiative aimed at providing affordable, well-planned, and legally compliant independent homes to eligible buyers across Maharashtra.

The project offers Maharashtra RERA Registered Independent Homes developed in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966. Designed to ensure transparency, security, and long-term value, the housing scheme focuses on quality construction, proper approvals, and buyer-friendly processes.

The homes are planned to deliver a balanced lifestyle with essential amenities, open spaces, and modern infrastructure, making them suitable for end users as well as long-term investors.

With a strong emphasis on regulatory compliance and transparent allotment, Maharashtra Special Housing Yojna aims to create a sustainable residential community.
